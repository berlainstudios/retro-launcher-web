Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the Retro Launcher app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by berlain.ui

Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide (Optional)

    • Premium Purchase: If you choose to purchase the premium version, we process the transaction through Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment information (credit card, etc.) - this is handled by Google.
    • Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and message content to provide support.

B. Information Collected Automatically

    • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect:
      • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
      • Usage statistics (which features are used, session duration)
      • Crash reports (to fix bugs)
      • IP address (anonymized by Firebase for geographic approximation)
    • Purchase Verification: To verify premium status, we check with Google Play Services if a valid purchase exists.

C. Device Permissions We Use

    • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES Permission: This permission allows Retro Launcher to display your installed applications on the home screen. We do not transmit, store, or analyze your installed apps list. This data remains entirely on your device.
    • Internet Permission: Required for analytics and purchase verification.

Does the Application use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies?

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data or provide features.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services:

1. Google Analytics for Firebase

    • Purpose: Anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting
    • Data Shared: Device information, usage statistics, crash logs
    • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. Google Play Services

    • Purpose: In-app purchase processing and verification
    • Data Shared: Purchase status, device information for license checking
    • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Google Play Billing

    • Purpose: Processing premium purchases
    • Data Shared: Transaction information (handled by Google, not us)
    • Privacy Policy: https://payments.google.com/termsOfService

We only share data necessary for these services to function. Firebase may collect IP addresses temporarily for geographic approximation, but these are anonymized and not accessible to us in identifiable form.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can halt all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

Your Privacy Choices

    • Analytics Opt-Out: You can disable analytics collection in your device settings or by contacting us.
    • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
    • Permissions Control: You can deny the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission, but this will prevent the launcher from displaying your installed apps.
    • Purchase Data: Purchase history is managed by Google. Contact Google Play support for purchase data requests.

What is the data retention policy and how can you manage your information?

We retain analytics data for up to 24 months. Purchase verification data is retained as long as needed to verify your premium status. If you'd like us to delete your data, please contact us at berlain.studios@gmail.com

How does the Application address children's privacy?

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. In the case the Service Provider discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, the Service Provider will immediately delete this from their servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at berlain.studios@gmail.com

For example, we limit access to this information to authorized employees and contractors who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve their Application. By using the Application, you are giving your consent to the Service Provider processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us. "Processing" means using, collecting, storing, or otherwise handling your information as described in this policy.
